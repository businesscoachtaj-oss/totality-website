FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Is the client app really free? +

Yes. Clients never pay for Totality. They download the app, accept your invite and they're in. They can follow their programme, log sessions, track food, submit check-ins and message you — all for free. You pay the platform subscription; your clients pay nothing.

Can I bring my existing clients and data across? +

Yes — and it's designed to be quick. Totality's guided migration wizard walks you through getting your data out of your current platform and importing it. Client profiles, payment history, memberships and attendance records all come with you. No data entry, no starting from scratch.

How does billing work for gym members? +

Gym member billing runs through your own Stripe account — money goes directly from your member to your bank account. You can set up recurring memberships, one-off charges, PT packages and marketplace products. Totality takes zero cut of any member payments. You pay a flat monthly subscription and that's it.

What is the auto-regulation engine? +

Auto-regulation is a training methodology where session intensity adjusts based on how a client is actually performing — not just what was planned. When a client logs a session with their RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion), Totality analyses their performance and flags underperformance to the trainer automatically. No other platform offers this natively. No AI, no add-ons — it's built in and included on all plans.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes. Every new account starts with a free trial — no credit card required to get started. You get full access to the platform so you can set up your gym, invite trainers and clients and experience everything before committing.

What happens to my data if I cancel? +

Your data is always yours. Export your client list, session history, payment records and programme templates at any time — before, during or after your subscription. We don't hold your data hostage. Cancel anytime from your settings screen, no exit fees, no phone call required.

Do I need separate apps for training and nutrition? +

No. Training, nutrition, check-ins, messaging and progress tracking are all in one app for your clients. As a trainer, you set macro targets, review food logs and see nutrition trends alongside session data — in the same place. One platform, one login for everyone.

I run a large gym with multiple locations. Can Totality handle that? +

Yes. The Enterprise plan supports unlimited trainers, up to 5,000 active clients and multiple locations. For groups above that, contact us for a custom quote — we scale pricing per client tier and include white label branding and dedicated account management.
