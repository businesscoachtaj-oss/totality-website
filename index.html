FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Is the client app really free? +

Yes. Clients never pay for Totality. They download the app, accept your invite and they're in. They can follow their programme, log sessions, track food, submit check-ins and message you — all for free. You pay the platform subscription; your clients pay nothing.

Can I bring my existing clients and data across? +

Yes. Totality supports guided migration from PTminder, Trainerize and Mindbody, and a CSV import wizard for any other platform — map your own columns and bring your client list, payment history and memberships with you. No starting from scratch.

How does billing work for gym members? +

Gym member billing runs through your own Stripe account — money goes directly from your member to your bank account. You can set up recurring memberships, one-off charges, PT packages and marketplace products. Totality charges zero commission on any member payments — you pay a flat monthly subscription and that's it. A small processing fee of 3.5% applies to in-app payments (POS, marketplace, and member billing) to cover the cost of moving money through the payment system — this is a processing cost, not a cut of your revenue, and it's itemised clearly on every payout so there's never a surprise. Stripe is included from day one on Studio and Enterprise; solo coaches can add it for $9.99/mo whenever they're ready to take payments through the app.

What is the auto-regulation engine? +

Auto-regulation is a training methodology where session intensity adjusts based on how a client is actually performing — not just what was planned. When a client logs a session with their RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion), Totality analyses their performance and flags underperformance to the trainer automatically. It's built as clear, coach-reviewable logic — no AI, no add-ons — and included on all plans.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes. Every new account starts with a 7-day free trial — no credit card required to get started. On top of that, new accounts get 2 months free for both gyms and solo coaches. You get full access to the platform so you can set up your gym, invite trainers and clients and experience everything before committing.

What happens to my data if I cancel? +

Your data is always yours. Export your client list, session history, payment records and programme templates at any time — before, during or after your subscription. We don't hold your data hostage. Cancel anytime from your settings screen, no exit fees, no phone call required.

Do I need separate apps for training and nutrition? +

No. Training, nutrition, check-ins, messaging and progress tracking are all in one app for your clients. As a trainer, you set macro targets, review food logs and see nutrition trends alongside session data — in the same place. One platform, one login for everyone.

I run a large gym with multiple locations. Can Totality handle that? +

Yes. The Enterprise plan supports unlimited trainers, up to 5,000 active clients and multiple locations. For groups above that, contact us for a custom quote — we scale pricing per client tier and offer dedicated account management.

Is the processing fee the same as commission? +

No, and this distinction matters. Commission is a cut of your service revenue — some platforms take up to 20% of what you charge your clients, on top of a monthly fee. Totality never does that. The 3.5% processing fee only applies to in-app payments (POS charges and marketplace purchases) and covers the real cost of moving money through the payment system — the same category of cost every payment processor charges. It's itemised clearly on every payout so you always know exactly what you're paying and why.
